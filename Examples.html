READING

Your chance to discover!
If you are here, that means you want to read more books, but can't persuade yourself to do so. Don't worry, we have got you covered.

You may ask yourself, why should people read? We have movies, music and thousands of other hobbies and ways to learn. Reading just means looking at dried ink on paper, right? WRONG!
Let me enlighten you with the benefits of reading.

BENEFITS

  1. READING MAKES YOU SMARTER
    • Builds vocabulary
    • Improves writing
    • Helps you understand complex ideas
    • MORE READING = EASIER LEARNING
  2. READING OPENS YOUR IMAGINATION
    • Books take you to places you have never been
    • Creative thinking
    • Experience without being there
  3. READING REDUCES STRESS
    • It allows you to relax
    • Improves mental health and focus
    • Teaches you empathy

Still not convinced if reading is for you?
Perhaps you need a more concrete piece of information.

FACTS

READING STRENGTHENS THE BRAIN

MRI studies show that reading increases neural connectivity in the brain’s language and sensory areas.

FACT: After 2–3 weeks of regular reading, researchers observed measurable increases in brain activity (small but significant).
READING REDUCES STRESS BY UP TO 68%

A study from the University of Sussex found that reading lowers stress levels by 68%, beating music (61%), tea (54%), and walking (42%).
READING IMPROVES VOCABULARY GROWTH BY 50–100%

Regular readers develop vocabularies 50–100% larger than non-readers.
